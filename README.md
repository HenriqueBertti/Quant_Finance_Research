# Quant_Finance_Research

This repository centralizes my quantitative finance research, asset allocation models, market reports, and machine learning development pipelines. It serves as a comprehensive portfolio of my academic and personal projects in quantitative risk, trading, and asset management.

---

## 📁 Repository Structure

### 1. [Undergrad Research](./Undergrad%20Research)
Focuses on academic research, statistical modeling, and quantitative risk assessment developed during my Undergraduate Research (Iniciação Científica).
* **Key Project:** `RiskModelling.ipynb` — Statistical methods for risk assessment, forecasting, and data acquisition via `yfinance`.

### 2. [Asset Allocation](./Asset%20Allocation)
Contains practical portfolio management challenges, optimization frameworks, and ETF analysis.
* **Key Project:** `ETFsAllocation.ipynb` — Implementation of Risk-Parity and Maximum Diversification frameworks with rolling volatility analysis.
* **Key Project:** `ETFsAllocation_2.ipynb` — Advanced ETF portfolio rebalancing strategies and tail-risk assessment (VaR/CVaR).

### 3. [Projects](./Projects)
Dedicated to personal projects, including advanced predictive modeling and machine learning applications.
* **Key Project:** `ML_ModelDev1.ipynb` & `ML_ModelDev2.ipynb` — End-to-end Machine Learning pipelines featuring data engineering, ensemble model training, and feature importance analysis.

### 4. [Reports](./Reports)
Centralizes the financial market reports, automated data collection scripts, and macro analysis developed for the university's Finance League.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Libraries

* **Data Acquisition & Handling:** `yfinance`, `pandas`
* **Mathematical & Statistical Computing:** `numpy`, `scipy`, `statsmodels`
* **Machine Learning:** `scikit-learn`
* **Data Visualization:** `matplotlib`, `seaborn`, `plotly`
